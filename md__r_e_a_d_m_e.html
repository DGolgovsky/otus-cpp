About the course

Being one of the most popular programming languages, C++ is widely used for software development. Scope of usage includes the creation of operating systems, a variety of applications, device drivers, applications for embedded systems, high-performance servers, and entertainment applications (games). In the course "C++ Developer" will be considered as introductory concepts, such as automation tools, STL, innovations of 11 and 14 standards; and more complex: asynchronous programming, design patterns, distributed high-availability services architectures.
